Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the minimum lease, payment security, and our strict receipt policy.

Yes, all standard listings on HosteLink require a minimum, non-negotiable commitment of one (1) year (12 months) of prepaid rent. This policy ensures stable occupancy for the hostel owners and a guaranteed room for the student for the full academic year.

The hostel owner determines the final rent price based on their property's amenities, location, and room type. HosteLink is a marketplace, and we do not add platform markups to the rent itself. Any platform fees (if applicable) are clearly itemized during the checkout process.

A PDF receipt is generated and available for a one-time free download immediately after payment. If you lose your original receipt and require a re-download from our servers, a retrieval fee of **500 Frs CFA** will be charged. Please save your receipt securely upon first download.

All hostel owners are required to submit proof of ownership and legal authorization to rent. Our team conducts a basic digital verification of the property location, amenities, and owner identity before a listing goes live to protect our student renters.
