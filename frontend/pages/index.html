Common Questions

How and when do I get my 500 FCFA agent fee?
Once you upload a hostel, our Admin team verifies the availability and photos within 48 hours. Once marked "Valid," 500 FCFA is added to your dashboard wallet, which you can withdraw via MTN or Orange Money.
Is booking a room on HosteLink safe?
Yes. We only push listings to the app after they have been physically verified by our team. Furthermore, your payment is held until your "Golden Receipt" is generated, ensuring transparency with the landlord.
What if an Agent uploads fake hostel info?
HosteLink has a strict verification policy. If an agent submits fake information, the post is rejected, no compensation is paid, and repeated attempts lead to a permanent ban of the agent account.
Why do I have to pay 500 FCFA for a second receipt?
The first download is always free to ensure you get your proof of payment. We charge a small maintenance fee for subsequent downloads to support the secure cloud storage and verification services of the platform.
Do I need to be in Buea to use the app?
Not at all! Students can book from anywhere in the country. However, our Discovery Agents are currently active in Buea (Molyko, Bonduma, Mile 17, etc.) to ensure local listings are fresh.
